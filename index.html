CS 1130 FINAL OUTPUT

This personal website serves as the final project of Kylle Wednesday T. Aguilon in CS 1130 - Introduction to Computing. It showcases a compilation of programming activities, including unit conversions (Celsius to Fahrenheit, Fahrenheit to Celsius, Feet to Meters, and Meters to Feet), an Income Tax Calculator, Arithmetic Loops (Factorial using a while loop, Sum using a do-while loop, and Average using a for loop), and a Simple Payroll System.
